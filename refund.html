Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last updated on June 24th, 2026

⚠️ IMPORTANT NOTICE — UTILITY POINT AND PREMIUM SALES ARE GENERALLY FINAL Because Kimchi Points and Premium priority flags are digital utility provisions that are delivered instantly to your profile's local memory parameters upon payment confirmation, we enforce a strict non-refundable checkout policy, subject to the limited technical exceptions below.
1. Digital Goods & Non-Refundability Brevity Companion deals exclusively in digital utility provisions (Kimchi Points) and digital service upgrades (Brevity Premium). These are intangible, digitally-delivered services. Brevity Companion does not sell, ship, or deliver any physical goods, and there is no physical product to return. Accordingly, this Policy (and not a separate shipping or physical-return policy) governs all cancellations and refunds for the Service. • Instant Delivery: Once a transaction is successfully completed through our payment gateway, the purchased items are instantly provisioned to your profile account. • No Buyer's Remorse: Due to the immediate consumability of these digital assets, purchases are final. We do not offer refunds, cancellations, or rollbacks for accidental purchases, buyer's remorse, or dissatisfaction with scoring outcomes and queue speeds. 2. Eligible Scenarios for Refunds We will only process refunds or manual point credits under the following strictly verified technical circumstances: • System Discrepancies (Payment Dropped): In the event that funds are deducted from your banking facility but points fail to credit to your balance due to database latency, routing errors, or gateway timeouts. • Non-Delivery of Paid Features: If a paid feature you purchased is, due to a verified platform-side fault, never made available or accessible to your account. 3. Refund Request Timeframes & Process To request a manual credit or refund for an eligible technical failure: • Reporting Window: You must report the transaction anomaly to our Grievance Desk within three (3) days of the transaction date. Requests made after 3 days will not be entertained. • Required Details: Your request must include your registered email ID, the date of transaction, the exact amount, and the transaction reference number provided by your bank or the payment gateway. 4. Refund Processing • If a refund is approved by our compliance team, the amount will be credited back to the original source of payment (Bank Account, UPI, or Credit/Debit Card). • Approved refunds typically take 5 to 7 business days to reflect in your account, depending on your bank's processing cycles. 5. Cancellations • You may choose to stop using our Premium services or leave your Kimchi Points unused at any time. However, suspending your own use of the platform does not entitle you to a pro-rated refund for the unused points or remaining premium time. 6.
